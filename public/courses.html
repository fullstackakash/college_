Undergraduate Courses

Our undergraduate programs focus on building strong academic foundations and preparing students for both higher education and employment. Students learn through classroom teaching, practical assignments, and projects guided by experienced faculty.

  • B.Sc Computer Science: Covers programming, data structures, database management, networking, and AI fundamentals.
  • B.Com: Focuses on commerce, accounting, taxation, and finance with modern business tools.
  • BBA: Develops managerial, analytical, and leadership skills for future business professionals.
  • BCA: Emphasizes on application development, web technologies, and software engineering concepts.

Postgraduate Courses

Our postgraduate programs are designed for students who wish to specialize in their chosen fields and develop expertise in research and innovation.

  • MBA: Offers specializations in Marketing, Finance, Human Resources, and International Business.
  • M.Sc Computer Science: Advanced learning in machine learning, cloud computing, and software design.
  • M.Com: Designed for commerce graduates focusing on advanced accounting, auditing, and business laws.

Eligibility Criteria

  • For UG programs: 10+2 from a recognized board with minimum 50% marks.
  • For PG programs: Graduation in a relevant stream with minimum 55% marks.
  • Entrance exams may apply for MBA and M.Sc courses.
