Life at My College

Our campus is not just about classrooms and lectures — it’s a place where students learn, grow, and build memories for life. From cultural festivals to technical symposiums, our students are always engaged in learning beyond books.

Every year, My College organizes events like TechFest, Cultural Week, Sports Meet, and Graduation Ceremony. These events showcase the creativity, talent, and unity of our students.
