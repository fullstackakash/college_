Admission Procedure

  1. Online Application: Fill the admission form available on our website.
  2. Document Submission: Upload all required documents (marksheets, ID proof etc.).
  3. Application Review: College team will review and shortlist candidates.
  4. Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be called for an interview round.
  5. Fee Payment: On selection, pay your admission fee online/offline.
  6. Confirmation: Receive email confirmation of your admission.
