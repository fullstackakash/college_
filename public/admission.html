Admission Procedure

  1. Online Application: Fill the admission form available on our official website or visit the admission office for offline submission.
  2. Document Submission: Upload or submit all required documents (10th/12th marksheets, transfer certificate, ID proof, passport-size photographs).
  3. Application Review: All submitted forms are carefully reviewed by our admission committee.
  4. Entrance Test (if applicable): Some professional courses may require an entrance examination.
  5. Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be called for a short personal interview or counselling session.
  6. Fee Payment: After selection, pay the admission fee through online portal or at the college counter.
  7. Admission Confirmation: Receive an official confirmation email and admission number once the process is complete.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 50% marks in 10+2 or equivalent examination.
  • For postgraduate courses, a bachelor’s degree in relevant stream is required.
  • Students awaiting results may also apply provisionally.
  • Entrance exams or interviews may be applicable for certain courses.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

My College believes in supporting talented students. We offer merit-based and need-based scholarships every year. Scholarships are available for top scorers, economically weaker sections, and outstanding performers in sports or arts. Students can apply for scholarships during the admission process by submitting supporting documents.

Hostel & Accommodation

We provide comfortable and secure hostel facilities for both boys and girls within the campus premises. Hostels include 24/7 security, Wi-Fi, mess facilities, recreational areas, and medical support. Students are advised to apply early as hostel seats are limited.
