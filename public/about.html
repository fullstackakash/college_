About Our College

My College was established in 1990 as a center of educational excellence. Over the past three decades, we have built a strong reputation for academic quality, innovation, and holistic student development. Our mission is to provide education that transforms lives and empowers students to make a positive impact on society.

Our Mission

Our mission is to nurture globally competent individuals through value-based education, research, and innovation. We strive to develop leadership qualities, ethical values, and professional skills among our students to meet global challenges.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier institution that fosters excellence in education, research, and innovation — producing responsible citizens and leaders who contribute to the betterment of humanity.

Leadership and Governance

Under the visionary guidance of our Principal, Dr. R. K. Sharma, and an experienced team of professors and administrators, My College continues to reach new heights of success. Our management emphasizes academic freedom, collaboration, and a student-centric approach.

Academic Departments

We offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple disciplines including:

  • Computer Science & Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electronics & Communication
  • Business Administration
  • Humanities and Social Sciences
  • Pure and Applied Sciences

Research and Innovation

Research is the backbone of our institution. Our college houses well-equipped research centers that focus on Artificial Intelligence, Renewable Energy, Biotechnology, and Data Science. We regularly collaborate with industries and government bodies on innovative projects.

Campus Life

Our campus provides an inclusive, safe, and vibrant environment for students. With more than 25 active clubs — including Coding Club, Robotics Club, Literary Society, and Cultural Forum — students explore their interests beyond academics. Annual events like “TechFest” and “Cultural Fiesta” bring together creativity and enthusiasm.

Facilities

  • Modern classrooms and smart labs with Wi-Fi connectivity
  • Central library with over 50,000 books and online journals
  • Fully equipped sports complex and gymnasium
  • Hostels for both boys and girls with hygienic dining facilities
  • 24×7 medical and counseling support
  • On-campus cafeteria, stationery shop, and banking services

Student Achievements

Our students consistently perform excellently in academics, sports, and cultural events. They have secured top positions in university examinations and won accolades in national-level competitions. Many have also represented India in international hackathons and research seminars.

Placements and Career Development

The college’s dedicated Placement Cell maintains strong ties with leading organizations such as Infosys, TCS, Wipro, Amazon, and Accenture. Every year, 90% of our eligible students are successfully placed through campus recruitment drives. The Career Development Centre also provides soft-skills training and internship opportunities.

Alumni Network

Our alumni network spans across the globe, with graduates excelling in diverse fields like technology, entrepreneurship, research, and public service. We host annual alumni meets to strengthen our bond and inspire the next generation of students.

Community Outreach

My College believes in giving back to society. Through our NSS and NCC units, students actively participate in blood donation camps, literacy drives, environmental campaigns, and rural development projects.

Awards and Recognitions

  • Accredited with ‘A+’ grade by NAAC.
  • Ranked among the top 50 colleges in India (2024 survey).
  • Recipient of “Best Innovation Campus Award” in 2023.
  • Recognized research partner with multiple IITs and NITs.

Future Plans

We aim to expand our global collaborations, introduce more interdisciplinary programs, and establish an innovation incubator to support startups and student-led projects. Our vision is to make My College a world-class hub for education and research by 2030.
