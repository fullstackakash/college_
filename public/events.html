Regular Activities & Clubs

My College believes in holistic development. Alongside academics, students can participate in a variety of cultural and technical clubs.

  • Cultural Club: Organizes music, dance, and drama events throughout the year.
  • Tech Club: Hosts hackathons, robotics contests, and coding competitions.
  • Literary Club: Conducts debates, essay writing, and quiz competitions.
  • Photography Club: Covers all college events and conducts creative photo exhibitions.
  • Environmental Club: Spreads awareness on sustainability and organizes cleanliness drives.

Past Highlights (2024)

In 2024, the college hosted over 30 successful events, including the *TechNova 2024* Innovation Challenge, *Cultural Week Fiesta*, and *Women Empowerment Seminar*. More than 20 colleges from across the state participated, making it one of our most memorable years!

Workshops & Guest Lectures

Throughout the year, guest lectures are conducted by industry professionals and alumni. Topics include Artificial Intelligence, Entrepreneurship, Digital Marketing, and Data Science. Students can attend these sessions to gain real-world exposure and career insights.

Event Registration & Participation

Students can register for upcoming events via the college portal or by visiting the Student Activity Center. Participation certificates are provided to all attendees. Volunteers interested in event management can join the organizing committee for hands-on experience.
